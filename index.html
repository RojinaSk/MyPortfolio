About Me

Hello! I am Rojina Sapkota. Inspired by the potential of genome analysis to transform healthcare, I am excited to work in projects that use advanced computational tools to decode complex genetic data for early disease detection and personalized treatment!

Projects

Single cell sequencing of TNT forming cells

A research project focused on single cell RNA sequencing of Tumor Nano Tube(TNT) forming cells in ovarian cancer.

Bulk RNA sequencing of three ovarian cancer cell lines

Performed bulk RNA sequencing and found the most significantly differentially expressed gene in the cell line under treatment and control condition.

Homology Modeling of alpha adrenergic receptor

Manual structure prediction of Alpha adrenergic receptor based on the structure of Beta 2 adrenergic receptor. Compared it with the Alphafold created structure to review the accuracy
